# Meowl

Meowl is an iOS-first social platform designed for pet lovers to connect, share, and discover everything related to their pets. Whether you're looking to showcase your furry, feathered, or scaled companion, meet fellow pet enthusiasts, find adoption opportunities, or engage with a vibrant community, Meowl brings it all together in a modern and intuitive experience.

> **Status:** 🚧 In Active Development

## Vision

My goal is to build the go-to platform for the global pet community by combining social networking, discovery, and community-driven features into a seamless mobile experience.

## Planned Features

- User authentication and profiles
- Create and manage pet profiles
- Share photos, videos, and stories
- Social feed with interactions
- Follow users and pets
- Likes, comments, and sharing
- Direct messaging
- Search and discovery
- Pet adoption and rescue listings
- Lost & Found community
- Event discovery
- Notifications
- Profile customization
- AI-powered recommendations
- Moderation and reporting tools

## Tech Stack

### Platform

### Architecture
